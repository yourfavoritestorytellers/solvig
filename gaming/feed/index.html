]]> Halo Infinite is a first-person shooting game which is scheduled to be released in 2020. It is also known as Halo 6 and is co-developed by 343 Industries & SkyBox Labs. Halo 6 is coming for Xbox and Windows 10 also. This new release will focus on graphical presentation with new graphic engine which the team build with long time efforts.

There are also some rumors that team is planning to introduce more RPG elements as compared to recent versions, but yet it needs official confirmation. Some of the fast forward info which you may like to know is that. Halo Infinite includes Forge mode, Micro-transactions, and more. Below are some precise details which an enthusiast will keen to know about.

Image credit: halowaypoint.com

When is Halo Infinite is Releasing?

Halo Infinite is planned for 2020 Q4, yet the exact date is unknown but there are some rumors for 2020 winter holidays. Halo Infinite is confirmed title for Halo 6 game release from Microsoft at recent E3 conference.

About Halo Infinite:

Halo Infinite basically is the next episode of Halo 5: Guardians story line while taking the legendary franchise in ambitious & unexpected directions. This new series is powered with new Slipspace Engine which helps to get some next step of gameplay graphics.

Halo Infinite Trailer 2019 (Official)


Here is a trailer for Halo 6 from E3 2019 with which you can take a overview about the game. Unfortunately, there is no gameplay trailer available at this time, but we hope soon it will be available. The trailer above is just like an intro to Halo Infinite and it starts with Master Chief, with a UNSC soldier. The trailer includes some loaded Cortana voice over and Halo Ring rest you can see yourself in the video above.

Do Halo Infinite allows MultiPlayer?

Yes, that’s obvious it is a multiplayer game but what features & functions will be available that is unkown at this point of time. At least on Xbox there will be a four-player local splitscreen feature with which a team can play simultaneously. But do not expect the Battle Royale Mode as 343 as Halo 6 do not have that.

Any Beta Version Available for Halo 6?

343 have some plans over that, but it will not be a full beta version. However, they will release flighting program, where different parts of game will be available for community to test. Currently, this testing feature is available for Xbox at first. But, 343 Industries also insists that the PC version will also perfect release however, testing version release date is unknown.

Are Microtransactions Available in Halo Infinite?

Yes, microtransactions will be available but there will no more loot boxes. Yet it is not confirmed that, what type of microtransactions will be available.

Is Forge Mode Available on Halo 6?

Forge mode is back on Halo 6, and this time it is developed by Skybox Studio which is the name behind Halo 5 Forge. They know there work very well, therefore they also know ways to work around Master Chief efficiently.

The new features of Forge offers some new capabilities. Although by default there will be no Battle Royale mode, however, by using Forge Mode you can build your own Battle Royale.
