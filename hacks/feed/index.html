How To ScreenShot Macbook – 4 Methods

1. Capture Full Screenshot on Macbook

  • Press & hold these three keys (Command + Shift +3) at the same time to capture the full screen shot on Macbook until you hear camera sound.
  • The screenshot file (in .png format) will be saved on your desktop with a name Screenshot-date-time format.

2. Capturing Screenshot of Specific Part

  • Press & hold (Command + Shift + 4) simultaneously and select the area you want to capture screenshot by dragging & using left click on your mouse.
  • You can also use ESC key to cancel your screenshot and start over again. When you release the left click screenshot will be automatically saved.

3. Saving Screenshot on Clipboard

  • Press & hold CTRL while using any of the above screenshot command this will save your screenshot to clipboard instead of .png file.
  • After that, you can paste the screenshot in any other app by using the command + V action.

4. Taking a Screenshot in Mac Preview

  • You can also take screenshots in preview for that first of all open Preview.
  • From the top menu bar click on “File” and look for the option “take Screenshot” hover the cursor over it and select the desired type of Screenshot.
  • Next, the image will open instantly you have to save it by using the file menu again. Enter an image name and format then save it.
