How to Download Facebook Videos for Free Including Private Videos?

Follow the steps below to start downloading Facebook videos from a given page, profile or group. We will also discuss how to download a private video from Facebook.

  • First step is to copy the video link from Facebook. If you have already copied that, then skip the 5 steps below and continue after that.
  • Open the Facebook Page which has posted the video
  • Next, from the left options pane click on “Video” tab and find the video by searching for it.
  • Open that specific video and click on the “Date & Time” stamp available right below the video title.
  • Now from the context menu click on the option “Copy Link Address”
  • At this step, you have to now use an external service to download the Facebook video.

There are multiple services available here are 5 best Facebook Video Downloaders you can use here. You have two options either use online website or download browser extension.

1. FBDown.net
Just paste the URL you have copied during above steps and click on the Download button.

2. KeedVid.com
It is another awesome website to download Facebook video when you have copied the video URL. Just you have to paste the URL like in previous website.

How to Download/Save Facebook Private Video?

If you are looking to download a private video then you have to do some extra work for that. Follow the below steps if you are looking to download a Private Facebook Video.

  • First of all open the Facebook Video which you have to download
  • Now press CTRL+U to open the code for that page. Alternatively, you can also also right click on the page and then choose option “View Page Source“.
  • The code will open in new window or tab, here you have to copy all the source code.
  • Now open GetFVid private video downloader
  • Paste all the source code in the given box and then click on the button “Download“

Soon you will get your video file available for download.
