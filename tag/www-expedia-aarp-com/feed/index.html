AARP Expedia Benefits When You Are Looking to Book With it

Introduction to AARP:

AARP is basically an abbreviation for American Association of Retired Persons. It is formerly also known as Natinoal Retired Teachers Association. The membership is not just limited to teachers or retired people, if you are over 50 then you can join the program. To enjoy the benefits of this program up to now over 38 million people have already sign-up with the AARP program. You can enjoy dining discounts, travel discounts, insurance offers, grocery offers and more.

With AARP you can enjoy discounts from thousands of brands along with AARP magazine. Beside to all above you can also get expert advice on various topics. Just you have to pay $16/year for membership, multi-year plans can get you a discount on that price.

AARP Travel Center Benefits:

If you are an AARP member then you can get access to travel center which is powered by Expedia. You can enjoy the following benefits with it;

  • Up to 25% discounts on car rentals
  • Up to $100 credit when booking cruises (Selected cruises only)
  • Enjoy up to 10% discount on hotel booking (Selected hotels only)
  • Zero fee for hotel change or cancellation
  • When book vacation flight + Hotel you can save up to $570
  • Zero fee for booking flights, hotels, cruises or car rentals.

Sometimes you can also get exclusive offers or discounts via email newsletter or any other medium including Facebook or Twitter. So, don’t forget to subscribe to these services.

Final Words for AARP Expedia Service

When we tested some other travel search websites we find that AARP Travel Center powered by Expedia always offer a cheaper solution. No matter the search is for airlines, hotels, car rentals or cruises. In short the cost of $16 annually returns you in just a single trip however, if you are frequent traveler then it will always worth it.

We recommend that you must use AARP-Expedia travel center when planning your trip if you are an AARP member. However, if you are not a member then you probably think about that.

There are more than 100,000 hotel deals, and 6000+ cheap flight deals updated on daily basis. You can also setup real time deal alerts according to your preferences.
