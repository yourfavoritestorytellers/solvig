]]> 2021 Tahoe is an all new Chevy Tahoe with a fresh look and comfort also. It is a three-row SUV and can be called as a shorter version for Chevy Suburban. The cargo area in Chevy 2021 Tahoe is now more spacious then previous generations beside to overall big passenger cabin. The rear suspension is now independent which aims to fix many issues inherent with recent versions.

In Tahoe 2021 you will get various driver-assistance options along with multiple new features including infotainment system. Chevrolet Tahoe 2021 is scheduled to be available for sale in middle of 2020.

What is Tahoe 2021 Release Date

Late Spring 2020

What is Price Range for 2021 Tahoe

Price Range: 49,000 – 75,000

*Prices are Expert Estimates only. Chevrolet do not currently announced the prices but it will be soon available.

What to Expect from Tahoe 2021

  • More spacious, larger and longer from previous generations
  • All variants will have 10-speed transmission (automatic) as standard
  • Optional diesel engine available (3.0-liter)
  • As said earlier independent rear suspension
  • 2021 Chevy Tahoe is launching of fifth-generation

As you can see in the photos there are new headlights & front grille along with updated exterior body. Moreover, the interior also have significant enhancements along with some new features. Overall Tahoe 2021 is 6.7 inches longer and 4.9 inches higher as compared to previous models. This in-turn helps to get more leg and head room in both third and second row. The cargo room also gets more spacious when you fold down second and third rows.

All new Chevy Tahoe 2021 comes with updated 2 petrol engine variants. The standard engine comes at 5.3-liter V8 and an optional one is 6.2-liter V8. Beside to that there is also a diesel variant with turbocharged 3.0-liter engine. All variants comes with 10-speed auto transmission. Although V8 engine are carried forward from previous models but they have new fuel management systems in place.

The rear suspension is now independent to give spacious interior along with enhanced ride experience. There are also some optional suspension setups to select from along with independent suspension which includes Magnetic Ride Control & adaptive air suspension.

New safety features are also available including front collision mitigation as standard option along with trailer camera system. Bling-spot monitoring, 360-degree camera view and lane departure are some other features you can get.

How Chevy Tahoe New Interior Feels?


All the knobs, buttons and interface looks familiar with some changes also but there is nothing significant beside to transmission controls. Transmission buttons are now dashboard mounted however, layout is same as in previous models. Optional 10-inch infotainment touchscreen is quite new with unique placement over dashboard (mounted on top rather traditional embedding in dashboard). Leg room is increased over 10-inches in third row, while it is almost 4-inches increased in second row.

 

Wrapping Up

2021 Chevrolet Tahoe is now more capable and biggest SUVs available now. Beside to that it is now more modern and even bigger if compared to recent models. It may give a real competition to other three-row seats SUVs available in market (Ford Expedition, Nissan Armada, Toyota Land Cruiser). However, a final opinion can only be made after full in-house test, lets hope for best.
