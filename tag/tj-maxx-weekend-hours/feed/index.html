About TJ Maxx

TJ Maxx is one of large departmental stores and is an American based Chain. Up to now it owns 1,000+ stoers in USA and operates as subsidiary company of TJX Companies. The headquarters are located in Massachusetts and now it recognized as largest clothes departmental stores in the region. The company starts its journey back in 1976 and the founder name was Bernard Cammarata.
