Macy’s Coupon to Get Extra Discounts

There are multiple coupons & promo codes available to get special discounts when buying with Macys. The coupons are updated on weekly basis, and you can get new offers anywhere from 10%-60% off. Macys coupons are sub-categorized in to multiple sections however, site-wide coupons are also available.

You can visit Macy’s Official Coupon Page to find the best possible discount offer. Beside to that you can also find offers on Twitter, for that just search for Macy’s printable coupons on your account. Weekly flyers are also another option to get Macys personalized offers & deals.

Macy’s often offer special discounts in email newsletters so, its a very nice approach to sign-up for newsletter. But here, a question arise in mind whether you should use coupon in store or use it online? Most of times the offers are same for both mediums however, there may be extra shipping costs when you buy online.

Note: You are not allowed to use multiple coupons on the same order, only one coupon can be applied at a given time.

What Other Options do I Have Beside to Macy’s Coupons

If you are looking to find some other ways to save with Macys beside to coupons then you can consider using the below methods.

1. Download Macy’s App

All the new app users get a straight 25% discount on first purchase or order. Beside to that there are multiple other benefits of using the app you can manage your profile much easier.

2. Enroll for Macy’s Star Rewards

It is a free loyalty program where you will earn points on each dollar spent and then can redeem those points in dollars. To enroll sign-up for a Macys.com account and then look for Macy’s star rewards. Once you enroll you will get a 25% off coupon for your next order.

3. Using Macy’s Gift Cards

You can use discounted Macy’s gift cards to enjoy same benefits as you would get when use Macys credit card. One thing to keep in mind is that, you can only get special benefits when you use the entire physical value of gift card.

4. Other Ways to Get Discounts

Find late week deals at store, typically stores have special deals on Thursday & Fridays. While some store offer special deals on weekends, you can try both or variations of both.
