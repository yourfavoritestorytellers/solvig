]]> Free Nintendo Switch Games are best options for people looking to play games without spending any single penny. We may don’t have pocket full to spend on entertainment in those times free games for Nintendo Switch helps a lot.

The portable console is quite popular among people, but most of us do not know that there are bulks of free games which offer very well gaming entertainment. Here we are sharing some best available games which you may be also like to play during free time.

Best Free Nintendo Switch Games 2020

1. Fortnite: Battle Royale


May be you heard this name, if not played yet. This is a popular game which offer battle royale mode where you can get 100 players fighting with each other. The winner is who survive till end of the game.

2. Fallout Shelter


It is a strategy game where the player have to design or populate his own vault. The game is available for both mobile & Nintendo Switch platform. You will love it so, don’t hesitate to give it a try.

3. Pinball FX3


If you love to play Pinball then this is good option to start on Nintendo Switch. There are multiple Pinball games available for Nintendo Switch, you can start with Pinball FX3. With free version you only get one table to start with, if you like to play more you can buy more tables.

4. Arena of Valor


This is an online multiplayer battle arena game where you can enjoy for free. Arena of Valor is best option when you want high quality experience without spending a penny.

5. Asphalt 9: Legends


Best option for people addicted to racing games. Enjoy your favorite racing car model including Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini and more. If you want a multiplayer gaming experience then it supports up to four players at same time with split screen feature.

6. Warframe


It is an intense third-person shooting game which also supports multiplayers in online mode. This is a free-to-play game which you can enjoy on Nintendo Switch for free.

7. Paladins


The game theme is quite resembling with Overwatch, however, if you do not want to spend money on Overwatch then consider free-to-play Paladins to enjoy same experience.

8. Lightseekers


If you love to play trading card games, but unable to find any popular names for Nintendo Switch then you might consider playing Lightseekers. This is an awesome experience for me personally when I give it a try.

9. SMITE


Another multiplayer battle game to put your strategies against your enemies. Select a God from multiple available options and win the battle to show others your skills & abilities.

10. Gems of War


This is a puzzle-RPG game while featuring fantasy foes where you have to win from them. Use your mind to place the best possible strategy to win battle from your enemies. Increase your ranking by winning weekly events and become master of puzzle in the world.

Closing

With the time multiple free games are going to hit Nintendo Switch. We will surely try to keep our users updated with any new or upcoming free Nintendo Switch games. So, stay with us and keep visiting this page for latest releases.
