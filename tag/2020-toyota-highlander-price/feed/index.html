You can opt for either gasoline V6 or hybrid four cylinder engine variants.

Toyota Highlander 2020 is certainly a better choice when comparing with older variants. Since there are many new features and design changes to differentiate the model from its predecessors. Although its not a very big SUV, but still it is relatively good option with dynamic look and feel.

Whats new in 2020 Toyota Highlander?

Now consumers have even more choices when it comes to three-row midsize SUV. 2020 Toyota Highlander is redesigned with features mix to get a strong position among other options available under same category SUVs.

This new Highlander 2020 is a bit longer and the extra space goes to the cargo area behind last row. The predecessor offers smallest cargo space in the class, however this time it is little improved but still behind its competitors in the market.

The quality of cabin material is improved in 2020 Highlander. On the front dashboard you will find 8-inch touchscreen as standard but in Platinum variant it is 12.3 inches. As an improvement or say new feature, Highlander 2020 supports Android Auto connectivity and Apple CarPlay which is really good thing for me at least.

Engine wise there are not significant changes, however now V6 engine is included as standard engine rather predecessor four-cylinder engine. That’s why you are able to get smooth and high-speed ride even when you have fully loaded your car both with passengers and cargo.

Although Toyota did a lot with Highlander 2020 but still there is enough room left to work on. That’s because there are some other newcomers in the market like Kia Telluride and Hyundai Palisade which are offering spacious interior with luxury options under same category. Overall, Highlander 2020 is worth to consider, but there are some other options too which must also be looked over.
