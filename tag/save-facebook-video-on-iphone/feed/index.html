Best Facebook Video Downloader for Android/iOS, PC/Mac

1. KeepVid

If you are using a computer then simply visit the official website for KeepVid and enter the URL of the video which you want to download. It is a free website and there is no need for account registration. Beside to that, you can also download the Android app and then can download Facebook videos in multiple formats.

2. Fbdown.net

This is another source to download Facebook videos with simple & friendly interface. If you are using Mac or PC just copy Facebook video link and paste on the website to start downloading videos from Facebook. Similarly, you can download FBdown App on Android if you are looking to get videos on your mobile device.

3. Savefrom.net

There is no official app for mobile platform with this option. However, they do offer a browser extension to ease the download process. Otherwise just you have to open the website on your browser and copy the video link and paste in the downloading box. After that click on the download button to initiate the download process. The service is free and there is no need to register an account.

4. Getfvid.com

It is another free video downloader for Facebook. There is no need to make an account, you can also convert the video in either mp4 or mp3 formats. Same like other portals, just you have to enter the video URL which you are looking to download and click on the download button. After that, it will automatically download the video and give you a file to download on your device locally.

5. FBdownloader.net

One of the best & simple interface among many Facebook video downloaders. The service is entirely free and you can download videos in multiple formats. Just you have to open the website and enter the URL of Facebook video then click on “Go” button. There is no separate mobile app up to now, however, you can simply download videos by using browser on your mobile.
