Top 10 Nintendo Switch Games 2020 Everyone Must Try

Best Racing & Sports Games for Nintendo Switch

1. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

It is basically a racing game if you love to play Mario Kart then fortunately it is available on Switch for free. You are allowed to add up to eight local players or can also take it online. Beside to that you can also switch between a list of available characters, rev up your engines and much more.

2. Fast RMX

It is a sci-fi ship racing game which features 24 tracks where you can also adjust the difficulty of game. If you like super speedy racing game then you must take a look over it.

3. Redout

Redout is another sci-fi super fast ship racing game where you can enjoy an arcade style action on your Nintendo Switch. Although its not liek F1 or Forza but I hope you will enjoy this.

4. Gear.Club

It is hitting to Nintendo Switch after successful hit on iOS and Android. There are 200+ tracks with online multiplayer option where you can switch between top thirty real world cars. Here is a quick overview from the developer on YouTube.

Top Best Fighting Games for Nintendo Switch 2020

1. Dragon Ball Fighterz

Being one of the popular & best fighting game available for Nintendo Switch. The game basically featuers a story mode with lots of special attacks, combos and much more to enjoy. Here is a trailer to have an overview on it.

2. Mortal Kombat 11

It is one of the best fighting game available for Nintendo Switch due to the fact it covers all types of gamers expectations. You can play offline and can switch into the story mode while unlocking gears on every sucess. You can also play online with other people worldwide.

3. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

It is a comprehensive fighting game which includes all the characters ever available in 20-year history along with instant access to 100-stages when you launch the game. It is really a competitive fighting game which can be played by both experienced and beginners.

4. Street Fighter: 30th Anniversary Collection

It is the ultimate game from Capcom since all the 12 titles released over three decades. It offers best of the class action from entire series which you most probably enjoy. It is worth buying the game for your Nintendo Switch.

5. Arms

Developed and published by Nintendo, the game offers some extra level of enjoyment and fun. Beside to that, it is quite unique game on Nintendo Switch where you have to beat the opponent with a stretchy arm.
